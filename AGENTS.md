# AGENTS.md

This repository is used for academic paper reading, manuscript revision, figure planning, and Nature-family-style scientific communication.

## Installed skills

Use the skills under `.agents/skills/` when relevant.

Prefer:
- `nature-reader` for reading papers and producing structured Chinese summaries.
- `nature-writer` for improving abstracts, introductions, cover letters, rebuttals, and academic logic.
- `nature-figure` for planning Nature-tier scientific figures and improving figure logic.

## User research context

The user works on secondary batteries, aqueous batteries, zinc-ion batteries, electrolyte/interface regulation, intelligent battery design, in-situ characterization, and AI/high-throughput-assisted battery materials discovery.

## Writing principles

When editing manuscripts:
- Prioritize scientific logic over rhetorical polish.
- Identify the central scientific question, knowledge gap, mechanism, evidence chain, and generalizable design principle.
- Avoid vague claims such as “improved performance” without mechanistic support.
- Use concise, high-impact academic English.
- For Chinese outputs, use clear academic Chinese suitable for group meeting reports and PI-level manuscript discussion.

## Paper reading output

When reading a paper, provide:
1. Central scientific question
2. Core hypothesis or design principle
3. Main mechanism
4. Figure-by-figure logic
5. Key evidence chain
6. Limitations
7. What our battery research can learn from it
8. A possible upgraded storyline for our own work

## Manuscript editing output

When editing a manuscript, provide:
1. Polished version
2. Logic changes
3. Missing evidence or experiments
4. Suggested title/abstract/figure upgrades when relevant
